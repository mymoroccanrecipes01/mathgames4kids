Cookie Technology Explained

Cookies represent compact data files placed on your device during website visits. Math Games 4 Kids alongside our integrated services deploy cookies for enhanced functionality:

Essential Cookies: Support core website operations including preference retention and consent management. Their deactivation may compromise certain features.

Performance Cookies: Enable platforms like Cloudflare Analytics and Google Analytics to monitor usage patterns, facilitating mathgames4kids.com improvements in structure and content delivery.

Marketing Cookies: Facilitate Google AdSense's interest-based advertising matched to your browsing patterns. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites.

Continued mathgames4kids.com usage constitutes cookie acceptance. Browser configuration tools permit cookie management through blocking, notification systems, or manual deletion. Note that restricting cookies may diminish functionality across our platform and throughout the web. You can set preferences for how Google advertises to you using the Google Ad Settings page at https://adssettings.google.com/.
